# Code of Conduct

Our goal is to make participation in this project a fun and friendly experience for all.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including but not limited to: repositories, issue trackers, pull requests, and community discussions.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and considerate of others 
- Accept feedback gracfully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Feel free to give others feedback, but remember to critique the code, not the people

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Personal attacks or insults
- Trolling, threatening behavior, or doxxing
- Publishing private information without consent

## Reporting Guidelines
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, there will be a method to report it to the maintainers but is not finished yet.
All reports will be reviewed and investigated confidentially.

##Enforcement
Maintainers may take action that they deem appropriate, including warnings, temporary bans, permanent bans, etc.
